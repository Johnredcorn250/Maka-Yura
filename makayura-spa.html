Our Story

A sanctuary born
from passion & purpose

Founded by Josephine Mpore, whose expertise was shaped across years in Belgium, Maka & Yura is named after those she holds closest — her daughter Maka and great niece Yura. What began as a dream to bring world-class wellness to Rwanda has grown into Kigali's most beloved spa destination.

Nestled in the serene Nyarutarama neighbourhood, our spa blends modern elegance with authentic Rwandan warmth, delivering an experience that is both globally refined and deeply local.
